Section: EXECUTION > Sealant

[Source: 07 24 00 - Exterior Insulation and Finish Systems (EIFS) (2025-05-07).docx]
Section: EXECUTION > Air / Water Resistive Barrier (Part 1)

Apply liquid applied transition membrane system centered on all vertical and horizontal sheathing board joints, exposed edges at terminations, inside and outside corners, etc. Apply dab of unreinforced membrane to all screw heads. Allow enough time as recommended by manufacturer before air/water resistive barrier is applied to rest of wall.
Install adhered sheet transition membrane at all movement joints, junctures to window fenestration, junctures with roofing membranes and at substrate changes (i.e., cement board to brick, brick to concrete, etc.). Transition membrane shall be of sufficient width to lap both sides of joint, crack, or transition a minimum of 50mm (2”). Apply reinforced, liquid applied transition membranes in accordance with EIFS manufacturer recommendations.
Where required, apply specified primer as per Manufacturer’s printed instructions and allow to dry. The primer shall be given sufficient time to dry, as per the manufacturers’ instructions and shall not be left exposed beyond the time specified by the manufacturer. The primer shall be allowed to become tacky before installation of transition membrane. Ensure transition membranes are installed on the same day as priming. Primed surfaces not covered by transition membrane on the same day shall be re-primed.
Consult with EIFS manufacturer
